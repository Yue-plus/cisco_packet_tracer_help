Devices and Modules: Structured Cabling Devices

Structured Cabling Devices are a special class of devices that are only visible in Physical View.

 

Copper Patch Panel

The Copper Patch Panel has 24 jacks in the front and 24 punch downs in the back. When doing horizontal cabling (between Patch Panels and/or Wall Mounts), only Straight-through cables are allowed.

The Copper Patch Panel is only visible in Physical View.

 

Fiber Patch Panel

The Fiber Patch Panel has 17 jacks in the front and 17 jacks in the back.

The Fiber Patch Panel is only visible in Physical View.

 

Copper Wall Mount

The Copper Wall Mount has 4 jacks in the front and 4 punch downs in the back.

The Copper Wall Mount is only visible in Physical View, but not available in the Rack View.

There is no real device representation for the Copper Wall Mount.

 

 

Fiber Wall Mount

The Fiber Wall Mount has 4 jacks in the front and 4 jacks in the back.

The Fiber Wall Mount is only visible in Physical View, but not available in the Rack View.

There is no real device representation for the Fiber Wall Mount.
