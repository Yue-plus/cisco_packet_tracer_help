Internet of Things

Included in this version of Packet Tracer are a variety of networked physical objects or Things. There are Smart Things that are Registration Server or Home Gateway compatible and can be controlled from a condition-to-actions UI interface. There are also components that plug into digital or analog slots on a microcontroller or single board computer. Programming in JavaScript, Python, or Visual Programming is required to manipulate the components. Additionally, it is possible to create Things. Things are physical objects and are only bound to the user's imagination. They can be everyday Things like coffee makers, microwaves or more complex Things like space rovers. The Things are programmable and can interact with Packet Tracer's Environment.

A variety of samples files are included in the Samples Folder.

  • IoT_Devices: basic introduction to all the prepackaged Smart Things and components.
  • iot: examples of integrating multiple Smart Things over a network.
